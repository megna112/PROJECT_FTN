Hunger Spots


Hunger Spots are the areas where the needy and poor people dwell with hunger. Hunger Map depicts the prevalence of undernourishment in the population of each country.From Africa and Asia to Latin America and the Near East, there are 821 million people - more than 1 in 9 of the world population - who do not get enough to eat. Our Hunger Map provides invaluable info that helps people to learn more about the biggest single risk to global health.
